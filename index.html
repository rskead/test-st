Season's Tales

Explore and take a scroll through the seasons experienced here in Ontario. Learn about First Nation's perspectives and teachings about nature, wildlife, and hollistic ways of life.

minoskamin

Minoskamin is the 'break up' season. During this second season snow and ice are melting so the people start preparing to transition with the weather for the next couple of months.

migiskaw

Migiskaw is the 'freeze up' season. This 5th season is when the waters freeze over so people would start preparing to transition with the weather for the next couple of months.
